→ Ping is officially closed

October 1, 2012

As of today, Apple's failed music-focused social network, Ping, is officially closed.

There are a lot of reasons why Ping failed, the biggest of which was probably the lack of Facebook integration when the service launched. Back in 2010, the word was that Apple was forced to pull Facebook from the service at the last minute, leaving the service handicapped. Without the backing of the world's biggest social network, the service never achieved critical mass — in fact, the only positive mention of Ping that we can find came right after launch, when Apple bragged of one million sign-ups in 24 hours.
